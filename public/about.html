Who are we ?

We are three CSE students creating an IOT project that aims to replace AC remotes using a simple web application and an infrared Sensor.

Why using a webpage for controlling AC ?

As you know always looking for your AC remote can be a bit of hassle specially if there are more than one person using it, It can be lost under a chair or lost between pillows. So by making a webpage we made it easier to control it with your Smartphone which almost everybody has it on him anywhere. Also, By making it a webpage you can monitor your AC even when you're outside your home if for example you forgot to shut it down before leaving or if you want to cool your home before arriving so that when you arrive it's already cool.
